STREAMFITNESS Site Plan

Site Name

STREAMFITNESS - This name reflects a dynamic and engaging brand focused on physical fitness, strength, and consistency. It will serve as an online gym platform offering resources for workouts, fitness programs, and health tips.

Site Purpose

The purpose of STREAMFITNESS is to promote a healthy lifestyle through fitness. The site will provide workout plans, gym schedules, membership information, trainer bios, and motivational content for fitness enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • What workout plans are available for beginners?
  • How do I book a personal training session?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #015C92 (Navy Blue) – used for headings and navigation.

Accent Color: #38B000 (Lime Green) – used for buttons, callouts, and highlights.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Segoe UI – clean and professional, applied to all headings.
  • Body Font: Tahoma/Verdana – for easy readability in paragraph text.
